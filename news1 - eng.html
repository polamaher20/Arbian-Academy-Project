Visit of the Measurement and Evaluation Center Delegation from Alexandria University to Monitor Exams at the Faculty of Computers and Data Science

As part of Alexandria University’s commitment to ensuring the quality of the educational process and measuring learning outcomes, the Faculty of Computers and Data Science welcomed a delegation from the University’s Measurement and Evaluation Center to monitor the final exams for the second semester of the academic year 2024/2025.

The delegation included Dr. Reham El-Gabily, Head of the Question Banks Committee, and Dr. Heba Galal, Head of the Training and Media Committee. They were received by Dr. Magda Madbouly, Dean of the Faculty, along with several faculty members, including Dr. Christine Raouf, Director of the Measurement and Evaluation Unit at the Faculty, Dr. Reem Essam El-Din, Dr. Mahmoud Gamal, and Ms. Shaimaa Ibrahim Zaki El-Din, Administrative Director of the Measurement and Evaluation Unit.

The delegation conducted an inspection tour of the exam halls and control rooms to ensure that the examination process was running smoothly without obstacles for students, in addition to verifying the application of necessary measures, and the availability of required tools and metrics to achieve the intended educational goals.

This visit comes as part of the University’s efforts to promote a culture of quality and ensure continuous improvement in academic and evaluative performance.
