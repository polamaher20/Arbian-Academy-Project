Faculty of Computer and Data Science Participates in the "You Are Stronger Than Drugs" Campaign to Raise Awareness About Addiction Risks

As part of its commitment to community awareness and promoting the mental health of its students, the Faculty of Computer and Data Science – Alexandria University – participated in the "You Are Stronger Than Drugs" campaign, organized by the Fund for Combating and Treating Addiction and Substance Abuse. The event was attended by Dr. Magda Madbouly, Dean of the Faculty, under the supervision of the Youth Welfare Department.

The campaign aimed to raise students’ awareness about the dangers of addiction and substance abuse, and emphasized the importance of early prevention. Students were introduced to the hotline service (16023), which provides free, confidential support and treatment for individuals struggling with addiction or smoking.

This participation reflects the faculty’s role in supporting national initiatives aimed at protecting youth and building an aware generation capable of facing challenges.
