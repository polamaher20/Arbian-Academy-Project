About the Faculty

Interest in data science has increased significantly in recent years by both companies and individuals eager to learn these fields. Data science has become an interdisciplinary field that relies on scientific methods, processing, algorithms, and systems to extract knowledge and insights from both structured and unstructured data.

According to recent reports, 90% of today's data was created in the last two years. Therefore, data science is one of the most widespread specializations today, and demand for it will continue to grow due to the need of major global companies to analyze and manage the massive amounts of data generated daily.

Based on this, Alexandria University decided to establish the Faculty of Computer and Data Science in its session held on 31/3/2019, with unique specializations representing modern trends aligned with technological advancement, serving the labor market, and contributing to building the digital society and developing its information infrastructure.

The undergraduate study period is four academic years divided into eight semesters, each lasting fifteen weeks, following the credit hour system. The study starts with six distinguished programs: Computing and Data Science, Business Analytics, Intelligent Systems, Media Analytics, Healthcare Analytics and Informatics, and Cybersecurity, with the possibility of launching new programs to meet market needs.

Dean's Message

The twenty-first century is the century of data in light of the technology and communications revolution. Alexandria University established the Faculty of Computer and Data Science to be the engine of progress on the tracks of technology, traveling the globe in moments by graduating cadres capable of creating and keeping pace with this tremendous development in all fields of data science, from health data to business management and e-commerce, from intelligent systems to the security of all data systems, and to media systems in all their forms and uses.

The Faculty of Computer and Data Science is one of the most important pillars of state-building and achieving sustainable development by focusing on data science, artificial intelligence, and distinguished scientific research. The faculty possesses a set of unique programs in technological specializations that have no alternative in other universities. With the strategic development of the faculty's programs, we will achieve the best results and provide the local and international community with competent graduates in all technological fields.

May God grant us success.

Prof. Dr. Magda Matbouli

Departments

Business Analytics Program
  • Analyze and model business processes.
  • Innovate data aggregation solutions based on modern technology trends.
  • Build data warehouses from various data sources.
  • Use different data analysis methods to support decision-making in business institutions.
  • Localize and manage information systems, including legal aspects, to support digital transformation.
  • Analyze data in various sectors such as digital marketing, e-commerce, and e-government.
  • Develop solutions and products based on data analytics technology.
Intelligent Systems Program
  • Identify, analyze, and evaluate components and fundamentals of intelligent systems.
  • Collect and analyze information and predictions from big data to assist decision-making.
  • Design, build, and test intelligent computing systems.
  • Design and build embedded systems and robotics software.
  • Learn how to design and manage smart cities.
  • Provide support, management, and maintenance to ensure the efficiency of intelligent systems.
  • Review and evaluate results to determine the effectiveness of existing systems.
Media Analytics Program
  • Analyze media content and anticipate digital changes.
  • Produce digital radio and television programs.
  • Analyze journalistic data digitally.
  • Develop media practices using data science.
  • Localize digitization in media institutions.
  • Build observatories to analyze social media data and sentiment analysis.
  • Innovate mechanisms for electronic opinion polls.
  • Develop media policies based on scientific analytics.
Healthcare Analytics & Informatics Program
  • Describe basic concepts in health sciences.
  • Use data and information in health fields.
  • Identify and evaluate health determinants.
  • Determine mechanisms for health decision-making.
  • Enable information sharing among care institutions.
  • Excel in managing healthcare systems.
  • Support public health and health research.
Cybersecurity Program
  • Analyze and design secure information technology systems.
  • Ensure the efficiency of IT systems.
  • Analyze and mitigate information system threats.
  • Investigate IT crimes.
  • Collect and analyze cybersecurity information.
  • Evaluate available cybersecurity information.
  • Provide leadership and guidance in cybersecurity.

1. Admission Requirements & Study System

  • The faculty follows the admission system of Egyptian public universities.
  • International students are accepted after approval from relevant authorities and passing required tests.
  • Transfers from similar and non-similar faculties are accepted.
  • Official language of study: English.
Study System
  • The program is based on the credit hour and semester system.
  • Two main semesters: Fall and Spring, each lasting 15 weeks.
  • Optional summer semester of 8 weeks.
  • Total credit hours: 140 hours.

2. Evaluation & Grading System

  • Maximum score for each course: 100 marks.
  • Final written exam: 50 to 70 marks.
  • Oral exam (if any): not more than 10 marks.
  • Fail if the student scores less than 30% in the written exam.

Study Levels

  • Level 1: 0-28 hours.
  • Level 2: 29-64 hours.
  • Level 3: 65-98 hours.
  • Level 4: 99-140 hours.

Academic Advising

An academic advisor is assigned to each student to follow up on the study plan and courses.

Graduation & Honors

  • Graduation in January, June, or September.
  • Honors for students who complete their studies within 9 semesters and with a GPA of 3.666 or higher.
