About the Faculty

Recently, interest in data science has significantly increased, both from companies on one hand, and from individuals eager to learn these fields on the other hand. Data science has become an interdisciplinary field that relies on scientific methods, processing, algorithms, and systems to extract knowledge and insights from both structured and unstructured data.

According to recent reports, about 90% of today’s data was generated in the past two years. Therefore, data science is among the fastest-growing fields today, and demand for it will continue to rise in the coming years, as major global companies increasingly need to analyze and manage the massive amounts of data generated daily.

From this perspective, Alexandria University decided to establish the Faculty of Computers and Data Science in its council meeting held on 31/3/2019. The faculty offers unique specializations that align with modern technological advancements, meet labor market needs, contribute to building the digital society, and enhance its information infrastructure.

The duration of undergraduate study is four academic years, divided into eight semesters, each lasting fifteen weeks under the credit-hour system. The faculty began with six distinguished programs: Computing and Data Science, Business Analytics, Intelligent Systems, Media Analytics, Healthcare Informatics and Analytics, and Cybersecurity, with the possibility of introducing new programs to meet market demands.

Dean’s Message

The twenty-first century is the century of data in light of the revolution in technology and communications. Alexandria University established the Faculty of Computer and Data Science to be the engine of progress on the tracks of technology, traveling the globe within moments by graduating cadres capable of creating and keeping pace with this tremendous development in all fields of data science—from health data to business management and e-commerce data, from intelligent systems to the security of all data systems, to multimedia systems of all forms and uses.

The Faculty of Computer and Data Science is one of the most important pillars of building the nation and achieving sustainable development by focusing on the fields of data science, artificial intelligence, and distinguished scientific research. The Faculty of Computer and Data Science at Alexandria University offers a unique set of technological programs that have no alternative in other universities. With the strategic development of these programs, we will achieve the best results and provide the local and international community with highly qualified graduates in all technological fields.

May God grant us success.

Prof. Dr. Magda Matbouly

1. Admission Requirements and Study System

  • The college follows the admission system of Egyptian public universities.
  • International students are accepted after approval of the relevant authorities and passing exams.
  • Transfers are accepted from equivalent and non-equivalent faculties.
  • Official language of instruction: English.
Study System
  • The program follows the credit hours and semester system.
  • Main semesters: Fall and Spring, each 15 weeks.
  • Optional Summer semester: 8 weeks.
  • Total credit hours: 140 hours.

2. Evaluation and Grading System

  • Maximum grade for each course: 100 points.
  • Final written exam: 50 to 70 points.
  • Oral exam (if applicable): max 10 points.
  • Fail if student scores less than 30% in written exam.

Study Levels

  • Level 1: 0-28 credit hours.
  • Level 2: 29-64 credit hours.
  • Level 3: 65-98 credit hours.
  • Level 4: 99-140 credit hours.

Academic Advising

An academic advisor is assigned to each student to guide their study plan and courses.

Graduation and Honors

  • Graduation takes place in January, June, or September.
  • Honors are granted to students completing within 9 semesters with GPA ≥ 3.666.
