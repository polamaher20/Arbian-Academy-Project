Launching the Final Exams for Spring 2025 – Bachelor’s Degree at the Faculty of Computer and Data Science

The Faculty of Computer and Data Science – Alexandria University – has started the final exams for the Spring semester of the 2024/2025 academic year, in a calm and organized environment, with measures in place to ensure the smooth and efficient conduct of the exams. The faculty administration has ensured the provision of all necessary facilities and support for students, while adhering to quality standards and supervision inside the exam halls, ensuring fairness and achieving educational goals. The faculty wishes all its students the best of luck and success.
