Invitation to Students and Graduates of the Faculty of Computer and Data Science to Attend the 2025 Job and Business Fair – Faculty of Engineering, Alexandria University

As part of the ongoing fruitful cooperation between the faculties of Alexandria University, especially the distinguished collaboration between the Faculty of Engineering and the Faculty of Computer and Data Science, the Faculty of Engineering – Alexandria University – is pleased to invite students and graduates of the Faculty of Computer and Data Science to attend the 2025 Job and Business Fair.

The event will take place on Sunday, April 27, 2025, from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM, in Lecture Hall (1) – Conference Hall, Preparatory Building – Faculty of Engineering.

The fair will run for three days (Sunday, Monday, Tuesday) and will include many important activities such as:

  • Training and job opportunities offered by major companies and organizations.
  • Workshops and educational lectures in the fields of entrepreneurship and skills development.
  • Direct meetings with employers to learn about job market requirements.
This fair is a golden opportunity for students and graduates to build a promising career path and explore real opportunities for training and employment in leading institutions. We wish all our students the best of luck and success.We wish all our students the best of luck and success.
